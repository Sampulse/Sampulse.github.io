How to Start a Christian Diary

Starting a Christian diary is simple — and powerful. It’s a way to grow in your relationship with God by writing prayers, verses, reflections, and even struggles.

You don’t need a perfect format. Begin with what’s in your heart. Here’s a basic idea:

  • Start with a verse you read today
  • Write how it spoke to you
  • Pray in writing — talk to God
  • End with a praise or request

Over time, you’ll look back and see how God has been faithful.

“Write down the revelation and make it plain…” — Habakkuk 2:2
